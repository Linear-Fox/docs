# Research API
> Research site is open source and available on [GitHub](https://github.com/Linear-Fox/ResearchWeb). This is a platform created for Linear Fox community researchers to upload and share their documents in PDF, LaTeX, projects, and ideas with the world. This platform provides an lternative to existing research publishing platforms, with a focus on simplicity, ease of use.
